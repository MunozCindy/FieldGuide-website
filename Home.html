Behind its Sweetness

In almost all cultures, bread has been part of the gastronomy, an in the Mexican culture, Pan Dulce or Sweet Bread has been a tradition forever.

No matter how you feel, it's hard to resist that marvelous smell of freshly baked dough luring you into your local “panaderia” or bakery. Before you know it, you find yourself loading up a tray full of pan you can’t hope to polish off; without the faintest idea of their contents or names. Not knowing what to expect from your purchase can be half the fun of the adventure.

This small guide about Pan Dulce will walk you through and show you why this type of bread is a staple in the Mexican cuisine. So, grab a warm cup of coffee and enjoy!
